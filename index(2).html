DRAWING

Drawing is a medium that offers an intimate and open field for imaginative elaboration, in which concepts and ideas can emerge and change with relative ease.uninhivited by the obligation to create a finished and independent object,as is traditionally associated with painting and sculpture,drawing as a medium lends itself readily to the theoretical and the exprimental.Notation:contemporary Drawing as ideas and process brings together over sixty works by thirty nine artists from the postwar decade to today .Notation is curated by meredith malone,associate curated at the kemper art Museum.the exisibition will be on use at the mildred lan kemper art Museum from september 14,2012 to january 7,2013.All art works in the exibition are on loan from the collection of sally and wynn kramarsky,new york,and the Museum of modern art,New york
